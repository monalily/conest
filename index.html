Work, Make Simple

We are much more

Co.Nest is more than another coworking space. It’s a place for working hard, having fun, collaborating and for getting it done. It’s work, made simple.


Our serviced offices and communal areas have been designed and styled to inspire you and your team.

Why Co.Nest

Co.Nest offers freelancers and entrepreneurs a chance to work in fully-equipped office without worrying about the cost of establishing their own office.

We host a range of events to help our members connect and collaborate. We are always thinking of fun and creative way to bring our community closer together.

Workspaces & Services

Private Office

Dedicated Desk

Flexi Desk

Relaxing Corner
